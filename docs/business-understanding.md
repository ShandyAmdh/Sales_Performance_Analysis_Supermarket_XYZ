# Business Understanding

## Project Overview

This project simulates a real-world business scenario in which a Data Analyst is responsible for evaluating supermarket sales performance using transactional sales data. The primary objective is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights through data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and an interactive Excel dashboard.

The project focuses on analyzing customer purchasing behavior, product performance, sales trends, and payment preferences to support data-driven business decisions.

---

# Company Background

Supermarket XYZ is a retail company that sells a wide range of daily consumer goods, including groceries, beverages, household products, and personal care items.

The company records hundreds of sales transactions every day. Although transaction data is continuously collected, management currently lacks an integrated reporting system that provides a clear overview of business performance.

As a result, managers spend significant time manually reviewing sales reports, making it difficult to identify trends, monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), and make timely business decisions.

---

# Business Problem

The management team wants to gain better visibility into overall sales performance and customer purchasing behavior.

Several business challenges have been identified:

- Sales performance is not monitored through a centralized dashboard.
- Management cannot quickly identify top-performing products or categories.
- Customer purchasing patterns are not well understood.
- Payment method preferences have never been analyzed.
- Product performance comparisons require manual reporting.
- Business decisions are often based on assumptions rather than data.

Without proper analysis, the company risks making ineffective decisions regarding inventory management, product promotion, and sales strategy.

---

# Business Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze supermarket sales transactions and develop an interactive Excel dashboard that enables management to monitor business performance efficiently.

The analysis aims to:

- Measure overall sales performance.
- Identify best-selling products and product categories.
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
- Evaluate payment method preferences.
- Monitor sales trends over time.
- Provide actionable business insights.
- Support evidence-based decision making.

---
